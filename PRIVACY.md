PRIVACY POLICY FOR SHOPEE PURCHASE GATE
Last Updated: December 17, 2025
Version: 1.0.0
Extension ID: 

1. OVERVIEW
Shopee Purchase Gate ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices regarding the Extension's operation on the Shopee Philippines website (shopee.ph).

Key Principle: We DO NOT collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information or browsing data outside of your own device.

2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT
The Extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or access:

❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)

❌ Payment or credit card information

❌ Browsing history or web activity

❌ Shopping cart contents or product details

❌ Purchase history or transaction data

❌ Shopee account credentials

❌ IP addresses or device identifiers

❌ Cookies or tracking data

❌ User behavior analytics

❌ Any personally identifiable information (PII)

3. LOCAL DATA STORAGE
The Extension uses your browser's localStorage to store the following data ONLY on your device:

Data Stored Locally:
Monthly spending total (numeric value only, e.g., "1500")

Current month identifier (e.g., "2025-12")

What This Data Does:
Helps you track your approved purchase amounts within your monthly budget

Resets automatically each new month

Contains NO product names, descriptions, or shopping details

Contains NO personal or identifying information

Where This Data Is Stored:
Exclusively on your local device in browser storage

Never transmitted to any server or third party

Only accessible by this extension on your device

Can be cleared by clearing your browser data

4. PERMISSIONS USED AND WHY
The Extension requests the following browser permissions:

Storage Permission:
Purpose: To save your monthly spending tracker locally

Data: Only numeric spending totals and month identifier

Location: Your device only (not transmitted anywhere)

Host Permission (shopee.ph):
Purpose: To detect checkout buttons and display reflection questions

Scope: Only works on Shopee Philippines website

Function: Monitors for "Check Out" and "Place Order" buttons to show the decision modal

No other permissions are requested or used.

5. DATA SHARING AND TRANSMISSION
External Transmission: NONE. Zero data leaves your device.

Third-Party Services: NONE. No analytics, tracking, or external APIs used.

Server Communication: NONE. The Extension runs entirely locally.

Data Sales: We do not sell, rent, or share any data (because we don't collect any).

Advertising: The Extension contains no advertisements or tracking.

6. HOW THE EXTENSION WORKS
The Extension operates entirely within your browser:

Monitors Shopee checkout pages for checkout button clicks

Calculates cart total by reading visible prices on the page

Displays a modal with reflection questions when total exceeds ₱500

Stores monthly spending locally when you approve a purchase

Never sends any data to external servers

All processing happens locally on your device.

7. DATA SECURITY
Since all data is stored locally:

You maintain full control over your data

Data cannot be intercepted (no transmission occurs)

No server breaches possible (no servers involved)

Clear data anytime by clearing browser storage or uninstalling extension

8. YOUR DATA RIGHTS
You have complete control over your data:

How to View Your Data:
Open browser DevTools (F12) → Application → Local Storage → shopee.ph

Look for key "shopeeGateData"

How to Delete Your Data:
Option 1: Clear browser data (Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cookies and site data)

Option 2: Uninstall the extension (removes all associated data)

Option 3: Manually delete from localStorage via DevTools

Data Portability:
Your data consists only of numeric spending totals

Can be viewed/exported via browser DevTools

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
